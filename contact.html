  THE MUSIC PROJECT FOR TELEVISION, INC.
In 1975, its film THE BOLERO, with Zubin Mehta and the Los Angeles Philharmonic, won an Academy Award for best short feature. Among The Music Project for Television’s many other productions is PERLMAN IN SHANGHAI, a film of Itzhak Perlman's visit to Shanghai with his students.

The Music Project for Television, Inc. is a not-for-profit 501(c)(3) corporation.
